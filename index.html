JØRGEN'S WEBSITE

Sorry if the visuals are unimpressive, doing it from scratch is more fun to me than one of those cool and sleek website builders.

Anyway, hello. I'm Jørgen, and it turns out that it's a lot easier to get a programming related job if you publicly display your ability to program stuff. Who could have imagined that, haha.

So that's pretty much my deal. I like working with computer stuff, and if I could do that while also being paid, that would be great.

But why am I making a site now, and not as a hobby during my studies, you may ask. Well, depression is terrible for your motivation. Once I have an outside sorce of motivation, like grades or wages, I can push myself to work very hard on stuff. But as a hobby, when the motivation has to come from within? Not so much.
No need to worry, though. My depression has loosened its grip over the years, and I'm doing pretty good now.
